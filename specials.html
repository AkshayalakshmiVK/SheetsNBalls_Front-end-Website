OUR CHAPPATHIS

Gobi Chappathi - The best seller of all time. Having conquered the hearts of the eaters, Gobi chappathi is the most selling dish of our restaurant. The stuffed Gobi inside the wheaty chappathi flavours magical when tasted. Have it with Dal Makhani, you will feel a heaven on earth.

Schezwan Dosa - What happens if Tamils and Chinese are friends? What will they have when they go for a dinner? Chinese or South-Indian.? Why confusion when our Schezwan Dosa is here. Known for its innovation, the dosa steals the heart of those who have it. They feel a strong feeling of their presence in ancient China. Wanna feel without going there? Try this out!!

Chola Poori - Situated at the area where the Pallavas ruled the lands.. But guess who ruled the people's hearts? Obviously Chola!! I mean.. Our Chola poori. It doesnt know its limits - from a toddler to a senior-citizen, this will be their choice when they think of ordering a poori. When coupled with baby-corn masala, their combo will beat the Laila-Majnu.

Panner Butter Masala - Should we need an introduction to this? In a rapid fire, when we are asked to name a gravy, this is definitely our answer. A standard sidedish for all dishes ranging from Chapathis to Naans, Briyanis to fried rices. The king of all time, Paneer Butter Masala is the King of our place.

Daal Makhani - Shouldnt there be a competitor for the King? There comes our Daal Makhani. Rich in protein and Makhan, it will melt your heart as it is 'Smooth like Butter'. This is the favourite food of our armies. Armies out there... Wanna try our 'Butter'?
